Bond....James Bond

Agent for the British Secret Service, MI6

James Bond is an orphan do to a mountain climbing accident. He currently is employed by the British Secret Service named MI6. This means he serves under the Queen of England. he currently has been on roughly 25 different missions excluding some that have been undisclosed to the public. One of his many archenemies is Ernst Stavro Blofeld. He is the leader of SPECTRE (SPecial Executive for Counter-intelligence, Terrorism, Revenge and Extortion). Another organization that James Bond has tried to stop is the Quantum Organization. James Bond has been married once to Teresa "Tracy" Draco. Tracy died fifteen minutes after their wedding.
